    Adoptive Parents are not always given the proper tools, and I have seen many confused and frustated. They look for guidance among other adoptive parents and professional when it the voice of the adoptee that sound valued the most RIA was created to help those seeking assitance when they most need it.

    RIA stands behind 3 things:

    • ADVOCACY

      We belive adoptee are the leading expert in adoption. We advocate for openness between adoptive families and adoptees and equal right for adoptees to have access to birth records, medical information and thier orignal birth certificate.

    • EDUCATION

      Education is the most empowering tool you can have. The more knowledge you have, the more confidence you will have when opening discussions of adoption in your home.

    • TRUTH

      Healing begins with truth. We passionately believe adoptive children should always be told the truth regarding their adoption stories.
